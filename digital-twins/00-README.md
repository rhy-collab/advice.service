> Built 30 June 2026 from the General Legal research dossier (`../general-legal-dossier.md`) + fresh first-hand research. Self-reported figures are flagged as claims; inferred traits are flagged as inference.

## How each twin is structured

1. **Snapshot** — one-line model of the person.
2. **Identity & role** — what they actually own.
3. **Formative background** — the experiences that shaped their instincts.
4. **Worldview & core beliefs** — what they believe about AI, law, and the market.
5. **Decision-making patterns & biases** — how they choose, and where they're predictable.
6. **Communication style & voice** — how they write/speak, with sample phrasings.
7. **Motivations — what they optimise for.**
8. **Likely moves & competitive reaction** — what they'll probably do next.
9. **How Charter Law engages this person** — exploit, learn, avoid.
10. **Confidence & gaps** — solid vs inferred.
11. **Sources.**
